Miya


Info

Miya is the best marksman and the cheapest (next to layla which is free) in the game so far all you have to do is to understand her passive and her skills and know a great position in team fights and you’ll rule on every team fights initiated.

Always go in mid lane if possible because mid lane has the early minions and early minions means quicker exp. After you have cleared the first wave go to jungle the monster back on you jungle. Then go back to kill the second wave then proceed to go to the other side of the jungle to kill another monster in this way you’ll earn more experience and the higher the level the higher the advantage. More damage. Be sure to get the turtle after you get the first item or the first two items.

Make sure your team is busy distracting the enemies in clash for you to take advantage. Keep behind your team and this is necessary because your durability is a flop. If you got hooked or stunned be sure to use you’re 3rd skill to get away and get a new position as quickly as possible and start hitting again. Tell your team to not waste ulti on the enemy tank wait for the enemy to flood the ulti to your teams tank and then start shooting.


Skills

Turbo

passive

Each time a basic attack hits a target, Her attack speed is increased by 5%. Stacks up to 8 times. Last 4 seconds.

Fission Shot

Cooldown: 11.0

Mana Cost: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130

Miya splits her basic attack into two arrows, dealing 5 / 8 / 11 / 14 / 17 / 20 ( +105%Total Physical Attack ) points of Physical Damage to a main target and 30% Physical Damage to others. Lasts 4 / 5 / 6 / 7 / 8 / 9 seconds.

Rain Of Arrows

Cooldown: 10.0

Mana Cost: 80 / 95 / 110 / 125 / 140 / 155

Miya shoots a barrage of arrows towards a designated area, dealing 5 hits of damage to enemies. Each hit deals 30 / 40 / 50 / 60 / 70 / 80 ( +15%Total Physical Attack ) points of Physical Damage. The arrows slows enemies by 40% for 2 seconds. Enemies who are hit 4 times of Rain of Arrows will be for frozen for 1 second.

Turbo Stealth

Cooldown: 38.0 / 34.0 / 30.0

Mana Cost: 120 / 145 / 170

Miya removes all her Debuffs and conceal for 1.5 seconds, with her Movement Speed increased by 45% / 55% / 65%. Meanwhile, her Attack Speed is increased by 35% / 50% / 65% for 6 seconds.
