Alice


Info

Alice, the Blood Demon Queen, is one of my most favorite mages in Mobile Legends. She is a very versatile mage. Through her skills, she may be a high AOE DPS mage or a semi tank mage. Depending on the needs of the team, Alice can easily adjust by picking out the right equipment. We will definitely tackle on best builds for Alice and how to use her in this Alice Build Guide for Mobile Legends.

Alice can output high damage from all of her skills. Each of her skill is able to deal AOE damage so its suitably great for clashes and team fights. Apart from the significant damage that her skill can dish out, she is also considered as a great nuker because of her skill’s low cooldown. Adding a few cooldown reduction effect from her equipment will allow her to spam her skills more often.

As a mage, it is expected that Alice is fragile from any incoming damage especially from burst damage. However, her third skill allows her to increase her ability to sustain damage by her skill’s AOE lifesteal effect. The more enemies affected by the life steal effect, the more damage she can sustain. Apart from that, her passive skill permanently increases her HP for every blood orb absorbed from killing enemy minions. Thus, it makes her more durable in late game. But again, she definitely has to watch out for any burst damage.


Skills

Blood Ancestry

passive

When a nearby Minions die, it generates a blood orb. Each blood orb Alice absorbs will permanently increase her Max HP by 10. After absorbing 12/25/50 blood orbs, she will receive permanently 10% CD Reduction/10% Magical Lifesteal/40 Movement Speed.

Flowing Blood

Cooldown: 6.0

Mana Cost: 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130 / 140

Alice casts Blood Stream in the designated direction, dealing 350 / 410 / 470 / 530 / 590 / 650( +180%Total Magic Power) points of Magical Damage to enemies. Using the skill again to immediately teleport to the Stream's location.

Blood Awe

Cooldown: 7.0

Mana Cost: 90 / 95 / 100 / 105 / 110 / 115

Alice deals 270 / 295 / 320 / 345 / 370 / 395( +90%Total Magic Power) points of Magical Damage to nearby enemies, immobilizing him them for 1.2 seconds. Enemies will be slowed by 70% after the immobilization. Lasts 0.8 seconds.

Blood Ode

Cooldown: 30.0

Mana Cost: 135 / 165 / 195

Alice enters the blood-sucking state for 5 seconds, dealing 160 / 190 / 220(+70%Total Magic Power) points of Magical Damage to surrounding enemies every 0.5 seconds while restoring 80(+30%Total Magic Power) HP for herself. If the target is non-hero unit, restores 50% of HP.
